Morten Spaberg is a Norwegian portrait photographer. His primary interest is in documenting the different people around him. Spaberg’s photos range from colour, black and white, analogue and digital, while primarily adhering to a vertical format. Photography is perhaps most potent when it conveys something about the world in which we live and its different people — that which would go unnoticed if we didn’t point a camera at it.
